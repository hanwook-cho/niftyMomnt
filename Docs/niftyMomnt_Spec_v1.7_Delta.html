4.5 Post-Capture Overlay NEW v1.7

The Post-Capture Overlay fires over the still-frozen viewfinder after every Still or Live capture. See PRD §3.9 for the full behavioral specification. This section covers UI/visual specifications only.

4.5.1 Overlay Surface

The post-capture overlay occupies the entire Zone B (viewfinder area). It does not cover Zone A, Zone C, or Zone D — all four zones remain interactive during the overlay window. The overlay renders the captured image as a frozen background with the active preset's vibe tint, grain, and light-leak layers applied.

4.5.5 Zone D During Post-Capture Overlay

The shutter remains fully active during the post-capture overlay. Tapping the shutter while the overlay is visible immediately dismisses the overlay (without saving a vibe tag if not yet selected) and fires the next capture. 5.2 Moment Detail Screen UPDATED v1.7

The Moment Detail screen is the single-asset view reached from any roll card in the Film Archive. It presents one captured asset full-screen with all associated metadata and quick actions. It is the primary surface for viewing, editing vibes, fixing, and sharing individual captures.

5.2.1 Zone A — Detail Navigation Bar

Height: 68pt. Material: iOS 26 Liquid Glass — background: rgba(8,6,4,.22); backdrop-filter: blur(28px) saturate(200%) brightness(1.12) + inner glow inset 0 1px 0 rgba(255,255,255,.06). Bottom border: .5px solid rgba(255,255,255,.08).

Back button (left)

40×40pt circular glass pill: rgba(255,255,255,.09) bg, .5px solid rgba(255,255,255,.12) border. SVG chevron-left (stroke 1.5pt, rgba(255,255,255,.82)). Tap: slide-right back to Film Archive, Spring transition 320ms.

Roll + moment title (centre)

13px/900 weight, letter-spacing .07em, color rgba(255,255,255,.88). Format: "[PRESET NAME] · [Location/title]". Truncate at 24 characters with ellipsis.

Share button (right)

40×40pt circular pill, lavender tint: bg rgba(107,78,255,.18), border .5px solid rgba(107,78,255,.36). SVG upload/share glyph in lavender #C4B5FD. Tap: UIActivityViewController.

Dynamic Island

Shows preset name + dot (amber if Roll Mode active). Same as Capture Hub DI treatment. Hidden when Roll Mode is off.

5.2.2 Photo Zone — Full Bleed

The captured asset fills from the bottom of Zone A to the top of the Glass Bottom Sheet. Width: 100% screen width. No padding or margins. The photo is rendered with the active preset's vibe overlays (grain, tint, light-leak) using the same Metal shader pipeline as the live viewfinder, applying them as static composited layers on the stored image file.

Photo Zone — Layered Overlays (top to bottom rendering order)

  1. Floating sticker vibe chips (z-index highest)
  2. Location vibe chip (top of photo zone, centred)
  3. Pagination indicator (bottom of photo zone)
  4. Light-leak overlay (right-edge gradient, blur 18px)
  5. Film grain (SVG noise, opacity .05, mix-blend-mode overlay)
  6. Vibe tint (radial gradient, preset-specific colour)
  7. Photo image (base layer)

Floating Sticker Vibes

Each vibe tag assigned during the post-capture Vibe Tag Prompt is rendered as a tilted sticker chip over the photo at a fixed position. Position, tilt, and border-radius values are derived from the chip's original post-capture prompt spec (§4.5.3) and are stored per-asset — they do not randomise between views. Maximum 3 stickers shown simultaneously; excess tags visible in the bottom sheet tag row.

Tap interaction: tapping a sticker chip opens an inline tag-edit micro-overlay (tag picker) anchored to the chip. The user can change, delete, or add a tag.

Swipe Navigation

Swipe left = next shot in the roll. Swipe right = previous shot. Spring transition, duration 280ms, spring damping 0.72. The pagination indicator at the bottom of the photo zone (pill + dots) updates synchronously. The bottom sheet content (shot info, vibes, actions) cross-fades to the new shot's data over 180ms.

Pagination Indicator

Horizontal row of dots and a pill: current shot shown as a 16×5pt rounded pill in rgba(255,255,255,.76); other shots shown as 5×5pt dots in rgba(255,255,255,.28). Maximum 7 indicators visible; collapses with fade for rolls with more than 7 shots. Positioned 14pt from bottom of photo zone, horizontally centred. z-index below sticker chips.
