Complete sequence from capture through share, APNs notification, inbox, viewing, reaction, and purge on both sides. All photo and video content is end-to-end encrypted — no content ever touches a Piqd server.

8. Onboarding Flow & App Launch Logic

First launch after installation runs the onboarding flow (O0–O4). Every subsequent launch skips onboarding and opens in the last-used mode (FR-MODE-08). Permissions fire contextually — never pre-requested. This section documents the first-launch path and the returning-user launch decision tree.
